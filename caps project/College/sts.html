COURSE DESCRIPTION
  • The Security Training for Seafarers with Designated Security Duties (STSDSD) course meets the requirements laid down

    Paragraph 4 to 6 of Regulation VI/6, Paragraph 6 to 8 of Section A-VI/6 and Table A-VI/6-2 of STCW Convention as amended in 2010.

  • It also meets the additional criteria specified in the STCW Convention, applicable to the issue of the certificate

  • The STSDSD course is conducted in accordance to the guidelines stipulated in the DGS STCW 2010 Training Circular no. 3 of 2013
